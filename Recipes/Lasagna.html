Description

This is a simple lasagna recipe and it is classic Italian dish

Lasagna is a classic Italian casserole made by stacking alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, savory sauces, cheeses, and often meats or vegetables. Baked until bubbly and golden, it offers a rich, comforting combination of tender noodles, creamy cheese, and a robust sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef until no longer pink.
  3. Add the onions and garlic, and cook until the onions are translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes.
  6. Layer the lasagna sheets, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
