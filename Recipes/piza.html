Pizza Recipe

This is a delicious pizza recipe that you can make at home. Pizza is a globally popular dish consisting of a flat, round base of leavened wheat dough topped with a savory tomato sauce and melted cheese (usually mozzarella). It is highly customizable, often baked in a hot oven and loaded with meats, vegetables, or other seasoning

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast mixture with sugar, olive oil, flour, and salt. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  7. Add your favorite toppings such as tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, and meats.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
